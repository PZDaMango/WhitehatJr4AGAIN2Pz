  • JAC Welcome Letters for April 5th Return

    JAC Gets News! Welcome letters from teachers to students are now available. Please take time to read through this letter with your child - JAC Welcome Letters Teachers will implement activities with their current students to help them with the transition during the week of March 29th. In some classes, this means saying goodbye to classmates who may be reassigned to other classrooms. In other cases, it may mean discussions about what full-day learning will look like, whether remote or in-person. During the afternoon of Thursday, April 1st, classroom teachers will meet with their "new" class. Timing and information regarding this special “Google Meet” will be sent from your child’s “new” teacher. The meeting will be an opportunity for teachers to prepare students for a smooth transition beginning April 5th. If your child is having difficulty with the transition, please contact your child’s current classroom teacher.
